Payment and contract

How we work with your order

1After agreeing on the main specifications of the product, we sign a contract with you.
2You make an advance payment of 50% by any means convenient for you: PayPall, Visa, MasterCard or by wire bank transfer.
3During the term stipulated by the contract, we manufacture your product.
4After the product is ready, we send to you its pictures and video for your approval.
5After the settlement of the remaining 50% balance payment, we ship the product to your address.

Shipping

Where do we ship

We ship worldwide. We cooperate with several international shipping companies such as DHL, UPS and others.

We ship by air, ground and sea, depending on the agreed shipping terms. You can also choose any other shipping company that will be convenient for you.

Shipping time, costs and taxes

Shipping time and costs to a specific country is calculated individually. The time of shipping depends on your location address and chosen type of shipping (air/ ground/sea).

The cost of shipping depends on packing weight and dimensions of the agreed product order, designation country and address, and shipping type (air/ ground/sea). VAT and other taxes & customs duties in your country can be included in the price of the product, provided that we ship via DHL by air using DDP Incoterms condition, otherwise your country's import duties, taxes and fees are never included in the product price and shipping totals.

For specific information, please consult with your local government import office or customs agent.

Warranty

All our products are made of high quality concrete only and undergo several control stages during all of the production stages.

We also check each sink for leaks and, therefore, are ready to provide 5 year warranty for the quality of our products.

The warranty does not cover the protective varnish on the concrete products, since its life time depends on usage and care.

Manufacturing time

We make most of our products customized, according size, color and concrete texture type confirmed by the customer. If you have a visualization or a finished product design project with dimensions, you can send it to us.

For each project, we calculate the manufacturing time individually, depending on the complexity of the product, and agree it with the customer.

Usually the manufacturing time varies from 15 to 20 days.

Installation

Petrobeton doesn’t provide installation services. The customer is responsible for arranging the installation.

Additional care should be taken when installing siphons on slotted sinks: don’t tighten with force exceeding XX N/m, as the outlet is glued hard to the concrete and may twist and thereby develop a leak.

Branded cool packaging

Each product is carefully packaged in a proprietary rigid packaging that we make ourselves, as we are interested in ensuring that you receive the product in one piece.

For packaging, we use a box made of sheet material with 30 mm foam inside. Packaging is included in the price of the product. From the moment of shipment to the moment of delivery of the goods to you, we give an unconditional guarantee of the integrity of the goods. If the product is damaged during transportation, we guarantee that we will manufacture and ship a new one at our own expense.
