OUR SERVICES FOR YOUR WEDDING

TOTAL WEDDING COORDINATION

Starting from the event concept to the production. From your first request we will lead you the way of discovering the best Santorini can offer for your unique.
rom finding the most breathtaking venue and deciding on a style and concept, flowers and lighting, our Total Wedding Coordination service takes care of every element of the wedding and is what we do best.

LUXURY SANTORINI WEDDING TOUR

“Marvellous Wedding” makes a “Santorini Wedding Tour” for brides who undoubtedly have big dreams of the most brilliant and stylish wedding Santorini can provide. A selected collection of the most prominent wedding venues and 2-3 of the wedding industry’s top florists, photographers and cake designers are ready for your visit. Without haste you can enjoy meeting and choosing them, all while sipping a little champagne during this eventful pleasant day. To move between locations you will be provided with a chauffeured vehicle.

DAY-OFF WEDDING COORDINATION

You started to plan your wedding day independently and booked a few services by yourself but you would like to have an expert planner to provide support before the wedding day as well as orchestrating the wedding day itself.

MULTY-DAY WEDDINGS

Weddings are often celebrated during a few days. Our team is here to prepare and coordinate delightful rehearsal dinners, exclusive group activities and farewell post-wedding brunches to extend and compliment your most exciting event of a lifetime.

Undoubtedly you are free to choose the wedding planning fee service and book separately all the relayed services as photographer, stylists, florist etc. And of course you think that it might be more personal than the package… To your surprise all the package of the Marvellous Wedding give you the choice to book the vendor you want, the venue you like and create your unique design! Our wedding packages are created to cover the weddings from the elopements till the big scale events with the pre wedding and after wedding activities!
