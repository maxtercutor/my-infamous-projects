HOME / ABOUT

Santorini is a love at the first sight! Touching and romantic… Warming and inspiring….

20 years ago I came to this island and didn’t resist this amazing beauty. I’m still living here and help loving couples to fulfill their craziest ideas for a perfect wedding.

My name is Marina. I’m a wedding planner in Santorini. For many years I’ve been meeting newlyweds who come here. I take care about arrangements of their arrangements of their upcoming events. And I’m passionate about my work because there’s nothing better than to look at shining faces and hear words of thanks from lovely customers.

As a result of work of the “Marvellous Wedding” boutique agency, we’ve created a small but a creative team. Our main goal is helping couples arrange their dream wedding.

The incredible azure of the Aegean Sea, smart snow-white houses with blue roofs, stunning views to Caldera... Your words will sound more beautiful in the unique atmosphere of this amazing island. We will help you fulfill any idea, however extraordinary they seem.

A perfect celebration needs paying attention to details. But you won’t waste your time in search of different experts. We’ll think through a wedding concept in intimate details and put it into life. At the same time, your dreams and wishes won’t be overlooked as well. Our agency cooperates with leading wedding experts. We guarantee high results, no matter what a task is.

Our team aspires to stress the character of every couple.

We are sure that there are not identical love stories. But there are unique ones, with their own scenarios. To make it easy for you, we offer a number of wedding packages. They depend on a budget and a form of the event. You may change them on your own. Our point is to give happy emotions on the most essential day of your life. And this is possible only when your dreams come true. Trust our team as 300 happy couples from different corners of the world have already done. Make any wish on your wedding day in Santorini and we’ll definitely turn it into life.
