ROCABELLA HOTEL & SPA IN SANTORINI

This 5-star hotel offers a terrific view of the volcano on the Island of Santorini. There are several terraces which could hold different amounts of guests. You can choose from them the venue that would be the best for you. Rocabella Hotel is meant to host high-level events for demanding guests. Here is also a SPA center. And you can arrange a reception here after your wedding ceremony in Santorini!
